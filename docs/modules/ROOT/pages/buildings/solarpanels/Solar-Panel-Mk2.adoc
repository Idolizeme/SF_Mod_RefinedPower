= Solar Panel Mk2

## Building Information

Generates 40MW during the day and some at night! Has a slider to set how much power you want to be maintained as an output! Has a higher efficiency due to its sun-tracking solar panel, which brings efficiency up to 69%!

Some Renewable power from the binary star system of Akycha!

## Building Cost

10 Batteries & 42 Photovoltaic Cells
